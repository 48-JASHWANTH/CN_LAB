COMPUTER-1
1) Go to control panel
2) Click on network & internet
3) Click on network & sharing center
4) Click on advanced sharing settings
5) In private networks make sure "network discovery" & "file and printer sharing" are turned on
6) In all networks make sure "password protected sharing" is turned off
7) Now create a folder and add the files that need to be shared
8) Right click on it and click "Give access to" -> "Specific people"
9) In the dropdown choose everyone and then click on add
10) Choose required permission level and then click on share

COMPUTER-2
1) In file explorer go to network
2) If network discovery is turned off then turn it on
3) Will be able to see available devices click on computer-1
4) Access the shared folder
